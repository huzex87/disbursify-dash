### 2. Competitive Advantages

**vs. QuickBooks/Xero**:
- Purpose-built for multi-business (not add-on)
- Nigerian bank integration native
- Naira pricing (3-5x cheaper)
- Offline-first for poor connectivity

**vs. Kippa/Bumpa**:
- Multi-business portfolio view
- Advanced reporting & analytics
- Bank sync automation
- AI-powered insights

**vs. Spreadsheets**:
- Real-time dashboard
- Automatic bank sync
- No manual reconciliation
- Mobile access

### 3. Moat Strategy

1. **Network Effects**: Team collaboration, accountant ecosystem
2. **Data Advantage**: Nigerian transaction patterns for AI training
3. **Local Integration**: Deep banking partnerships
4. **Brand**: "The app for multi-business Nigerians"

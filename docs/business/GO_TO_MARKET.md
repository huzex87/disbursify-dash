## 1. Market Entry Strategy

### 1.1 Phase 1: Lagos Launch (Months 1-3)

**Target**: 100 paying customers

**Channels**:
- Business WhatsApp communities
- Co-working spaces (Lagos, Lekki, VI)
- Entrepreneur meetups

**Tactics**:
- Free 30-day trial
- Referral incentives (₦5K credit)
- Founder-led sales calls

### 1.2 Phase 2: Nigeria Expansion (Months 4-9)

**Target**: 500 customers

**Channels**:
- Digital ads (Google, Facebook, Instagram)
- Content marketing (Blog, YouTube)
- Podcast sponsorships

**Partnerships**:
- Business registration services
- Co-working chains (Venia, Leadspace)

### 1.3 Phase 3: West Africa (Month 10+)

**Markets**: Ghana, Kenya, South Africa

**Approach**:
- Local partnerships
- Currency support expansion
- Localized banking integrations
